# DWProx V3 Installation

![](/static/assets/banners/whg_v3install.png)

Our free Access Control System.

!!!
Before getting started, insert the latest version of this product from [Toolbox](https://create.roblox.com/store/asset/4998244735/DWProx-Access-Control) into your game.
!!!

---

=== 1. Insert your file.
This product is free to use, it means it can be inserted via [Toolbox](https://create.roblox.com/store/asset/4998244735/DWProx-Access-Control) directly in Studio.
===

=== 2. Placement & Positioning.
You are free to move, rotate and scale your door and reader models to your liking, V3 comes with 2 industrial and 3 office doors as a showcase.

!!!warning
We don't recommend modifying reader models, unless you know what you're doing. **Proceed at your own risk.**
!!!
===

=== 3. Configure your V3.
V3 was designed to work out of the box, but for the best experience we recommend customizing the per-door config.

!!!info
For more information check out the [Configuration](/dwpv3/configuration.md) page.
!!!
===

Not working? Make sure you've followed all the steps correctly, or visit our [FAQ Page](/faq.md) for help, or contact Whitehill Support via our [Discord server](https://discord.whitehill.group/) for further assistance.

!!!
