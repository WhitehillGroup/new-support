# Updating Your Freestyle

![](/static/assets/banners/whg_freestyleupdate.png)

Keeping your machines clean and working.

!!!info
This product is in BETA, everything is subject to change.
!!!
!!!
Before getting started, download the latest version of this product from [Axon](https://axon.whitehill.group).
!!!
!!!warning
This page assumes familiarity with Roblox Studio's Explorer and basic model hierarchy manipulation.
!!!

---

=== Step 1 - Preparations
- Duplicate your `JSM | Freestyle Drinks Machine` model and store it outside your workspace.

In case anything goes south, you will always have a restore point.
===

=== Step 2 - Updating
- From the fresh copy of JSM Freestyle, copy the `Controller` script. (Inside `JSM | Freestyle Drinks Machine`)
- In your existing installation, delete the old `Controller` script, and paste in the new `Controller` script.
===

=== Step 3 - Test
After updating, confirm that the machine works without throwing any errors to the Console.

!!!
In case any errors occur, check the release notes in [**#product-updates**](https://ptb.discord.com/channels/616745092279173151/1169430975911043212) for any special instructions.
!!!
===
